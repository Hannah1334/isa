Detailed Information

Appearance
Physique/Build: She's tall for the average humanoid female, but not to an extreme. Despite her height she's very thin and lithe, retaining a shapely hourglass figure without having too much in the chest or rear to weigh her down. Hard muscle underlies her pale flesh and is even a little visible in most cases giving her the look of an athlete trained for agility and and endurance.
Hair/Facial: Silver hair, the length and style of which varies frequently, most often finds itself tied back for the sake of being kept out of her way. Bangs and stubborn strands still find themselves framing her soft features. Isa has a strong angular jaw, but it is softened by her rounded cheeks, giving her head more of an upside-down teardrop shape. For those that can stand to be in her presence the finer details of her facial structure are found to be quite pretty and fulfilling, as a cute button nose sits at its very center, accented by a pair of full rosy pink lips and big wonder-filled liquid silver eyes. Even her lashes and slender brows are colored the same chromatic hue shared by her irises and hair.

Personality
Isa is most certainly a hard woman to read. When she is not in danger, her demeanor is very child-like to the extreme. Many question her mental development and find themselves wondering if she is "slow" or "special needs." However, when extreme pressure is applied, especially when breaking under it or messing up would lead to her own death, Isa becomes a very cold and calculating individual. Something seems to snap behind her dull eyes, igniting a fierce cleverness often hidden or at least turned off for most of her down time.

Passive Abilities of the Gray

Biological Process
The anomaly of her physical existence ages much like the common mortal man, until reaching maturity. Once her body and mind have accomplished their peak development, instead of deteriorating she simply stops growing or aging altogether. Isa is still as vulnerable as most mortals, capable from dying to any natural cause save for old age.

Muscle Mass
Her muscles provide twice the durability and strength of the average human’s, but for half as much mass and weight. If she were thin and average in weight, at her base and in an untrained state, her strength would still surpass others of her build. Isa’s body is trained, however, her muscles especially. Not to the extreme, but enough to make a marginal difference and rival even professional weightlifters and bodybuilders.

Bone Composition
The durability of her skeletal structure far surpasses that of any human comparison. It is rumored to rival some of the strongest materials known to--and readily available to--mankind.

Enhanced Dexterity & Flexibility
Her capacity for staying upright and balancing on all kinds of surfaces is incredible, as well as her ability to contort her body into various shapes. She can bend herself and flip about like a trained acrobatic contortionist.

Adaptability
Isa is built for survival, allowing her to change in ways that can be argued as a minor form of evolution. This mostly has to do with environmental changes, like being able to keep warm enough to survive easily in freezing climates with minimal clothing, or keeping cool in searingly warm environments. Another good example is her flesh being able to adapt to the intensity of direct sunlight depending on her location, preventing things like sunburns as a result. If there are any extremes different than what she is used to in her present environment, given enough time in complete exposure to the new terrain and atmosphere will trigger the chemical changes in her makeup necessary for survival. This is especially useful for adapting to be able to channel and harnass the most common forms of energy abundant in any reality she comes across. While the act of channeling itself isn't a passive, her body's ability to adapt to the necessary conditions for surviving while harnassing that power is.

Glitched Existence
Being a sentient piece of such perfect balance, a literal embodiment of a moral grey area some may say, allows her to fade easily into the background of her surroundings so to speak. No, she does not camouflage, it is just easier for the untrained eye to slip past her as though she were never there to begin with. That being said, anyone who is not used to her presence (or anyone who finds her in the process of adapting before having fit herself into their native environment) and actually pays her mind will be met with malicious headaches and nausea. Her entire existence feels very wrong to natural instincts, as though she is some kind of abomination, and adverse effects vary greatly depending on one’s exposure to the otherworldly. Those with none whatsoever could even be triggered into seizing at first, since her presence comes off as so intruding and almost destructive without any actual intent to harm.

The Gray Legacy

The legends regarding the creation of the Grays have elements that vary depending on the culture it originates from. Some say the first was begotten by an angel and a demon, others claim it was a pair of gods from completely separate religions and realities. Yet across the board all agree it was an equal force of light and darkness, or lawful good and lawful evil, that conceived such a being. All of the very particular conditions were miraculously met, and from them the very first Gray was born.

This particular Abomination made her home mostly in the Between, the space between the walls separating realities and worlds across the multiverse, and wandered existence in search of a purpose. The entity that fathered her led her down a manipulated path, promising her a life calling if she completed tasks for him like a personal mercenary for hire. As expected, the first Gray was commanded to do all the dirty work.

Eventually her sire wanted her to reproduce and create an army of little servants, intending to use their worship as a means to ascend further in power, but every single attempt ended in miscarriages and infants who lasted two days outside of the womb at the most. It became obvious that another of her own kind would be needed to make a child successfully. There were no other Grays to speak of, she was the first and only one, so he hired a group of progressive individuals to experiment cloning in any method they could manage. Eventually a male counterpart was created from her own genes. By working together they managed to escape their prison. Most assume that is the end of their story, that they died off somewhere out there and have grown extinct.

How Isa Came To Be

It was easy for the original two creatures to hide in the Between, they survived there for some time, only sneaking back into the defined realities when absolutely necessary. The pair thrived and eventually did as they were designed, and the first gave birth to a daughter of her own.

Isa was an odd one from the start, behaving more like an animal on base instinct than a humanoid. She was incredibly smart and crafty, but lacked a lot of emotional self control. Communication skills were lacking severely as well, and no matter how they attempted to educate her, their training never seemed to work, and the lessons just wouldn’t stick.

The older she got, the more they allowed her to venture out of the Between with them, but her curiosity eventually compromised their low profile. They had just enough time to hide their child before being locked in vicious fights that eventually ended their lives. No one bothered to search very long for the identity of a child they didn’t even know, leaving her to wander the existences at a very young and vulnerable age. She spent most of her years paranoid, the experiences of mess up after mess up teaching her the skills she needed to adapt to in order to keep herself safe and alive.

During another journey across the realms, thanks to her nomadic lifestyle keeping her constantly on the move, she happened across a world where magic was the most common and powerful form of energy. There she met a young being with minor canine traits whose race of birth specialized in the magic of illusion and trickery. His existence fascinated each other, and their friendship consisted mostly of her following him around and observing his life with earnest. The two came to love each other, and after some time Isa finally settled down permanently at his side as his eternal mate. She remains a member of his family to this day, using her abilities for her new relatives when necessary, and enjoying the occasional adventure or two.

Eternal Mate

Isa's travels have led her far and wide across the multiverse, and throughout all the different beings she'd ever met only one had managed to hold her heart permanently. His world and way of life were completely foreign to her, but she gladly settled down and grew to know his ways. The perfect caretaker and etneral mate for her was hidden within the realms of magic all along! Who knew, right?
